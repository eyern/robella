Who We Are?

Robella Group Limited was incorporated by two visionary businessmen who shared a common goal of transforming the cleaning and Fumigation industry in Kenya. Drawing inspiration from years of experience in the hospitality and service sectors, they identified the need for a professional, reliable, and client-focused cleaning company that goes beyond the traditional approach.
