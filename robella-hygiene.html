Welcome to Robella Hygiene Services

Robella Hygne Services Limited was incorporated in 2022 by two visionary businessmen who shared a common goal of transforming the cleaning and Fumigation industry in Kenya. Drawing inspiration from years of experience in the hospitality and service sectors, they identified the need for a professional, reliable, and client-focused cleaning company that goes beyond the traditional approach. At Robella, we pride ourselves on delivering tailored solutions in general cleaning, sanitation, and fumigation, using modern techniques, eco-friendly products, and well-trained staff. Our mission is to raise the standards of hygiene while ensuring healthier and safer environments for our clients — whether in corporate offices, hotels, residential complexes, or industrial facilities.
