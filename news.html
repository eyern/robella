Our Official Recruitment Process

Please be aware of the following facts regarding legitimate recruitment at ROBELLA GROUP:

Official Communication:

All official job-related emails from Robella Group will always originate from info@robellagroup.com. We do not use free web-based email accounts like Gmail, Hotmail, or Yahoo for recruitment.

Official Platforms:

Legitimate job openings are only posted on our official careers website at [companywebsite.com] and our verified LinkedIn page. We do not make unsolicited job offers via text, WhatsApp, or other social media channels.

No Upfront Fees:

Robella Group will never request funds, require payment for equipment, training, immigration, legal fees, or demand the transfer of money at any stage of the recruitment process.

Confidential Information:

We will only request sensitive personal information (like bank account details for payroll) after a verified offer of employment has been formally accepted by the candidate and only through secure, official onboarding channels

Interviews:

Our recruitment process involves structured phone, video, or in-person interviews. We do not conduct interviews solely via text or instant messaging chat rooms.
